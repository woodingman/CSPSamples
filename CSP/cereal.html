Cereal App

This is your final Project - Play around with it a bit. What are the inputs and what are the outputs?

All the data is take directly from this Spreadsheet

On App Lab, you will need to import this data set and create an app that filters the list into a subset of items that meets the users requirements. You must have at least two filter and use the following programming techniques

  • Function with Parameter (from user)
  • Conditionals (Selection)
  • Input (Table of Cereal Values) and Output Arrays (Filtered List)
  • Iteration

This will culminate in you turning in Three Separate Products

  1. A link to your app with comments
  2. A written response Template
  3. A video of the app running with two different user inputs
